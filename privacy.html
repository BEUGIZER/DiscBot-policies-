DiscBot Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DiscBot ("Bot", "Service", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, protects, and handles information when users interact with the Bot through Discord servers.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the features used, DiscBot may collect and process certain information required for functionality and operation.

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Message IDs
  • Command usage data
  • Moderation logs
  • Server configuration settings
  • Temporary AI interaction data
  • Error and diagnostic logs

DiscBot does not intentionally collect highly sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment details, or private credentials.

2. How Information Is Used

Collected information may be used for:

  • Providing bot functionality
  • Executing moderation systems
  • Saving server configurations
  • Processing commands
  • Improving performance and reliability
  • Preventing abuse and malicious activity
  • Monitoring errors and debugging issues
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Operating AI-powered systems and features

3. Message Content Processing

DiscBot may temporarily process message content when required for specific features such as:

  • Moderation filters
  • AI commands
  • Auto-response systems
  • Logging systems
  • Security and anti-spam systems

Unless explicitly required for functionality, message content is generally not permanently stored.

4. Data Storage

Information collected by DiscBot may be stored:

  • In temporary memory systems
  • In secure databases
  • On cloud hosting infrastructure
  • In encrypted storage environments

Reasonable security measures are implemented to help protect stored information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing

DiscBot does NOT sell user data.

Information may only be shared:

  • When required by law
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the integrity and security of the Service
  • With trusted infrastructure or hosting providers
  • To investigate abuse, fraud, or malicious activity

6. Third-Party Services

DiscBot may rely on third-party platforms and providers including:

  • Discord
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Database providers
  • AI service providers
  • Analytics and monitoring tools

These third-party services may process certain information according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide bot functionality
  • Maintain server settings
  • Ensure platform security
  • Prevent abuse and violations
  • Comply with legal obligations

Unused or unnecessary information may be deleted periodically.

8. User Rights

Users and server owners may request:

  • Removal of stored server data
  • Deletion of configuration information
  • Information regarding stored data where applicable

Requests may require verification before action is taken.

9. Children's Privacy

DiscBot is not intended for users below the minimum age required by Discord or applicable laws.

If unauthorized data collection involving minors is discovered, reasonable efforts may be made to remove the information.

10. Security Measures

DiscBot uses reasonable administrative, technical, and security measures to help protect data.

These measures may include:

  • Restricted system access
  • Secure infrastructure
  • Monitoring systems
  • Encrypted communications where applicable
  • Abuse prevention systems

11. International Data Processing

Data may be processed or stored in multiple countries depending on the hosting infrastructure used by DiscBot.

By using the Service, you consent to such data processing and transfers where applicable.

12. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of DiscBot after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

13. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns, please contact the official DiscBot support team.
